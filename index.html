Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (split black gram)
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves

Steps to Prepare

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind soaked rice and dal to a smooth batter.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Prepare potato masala by saut√©ing onions, spices, and boiled potatoes.
  5. Heat a tawa and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thin.
  6. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden.
  7. Place potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  8. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (mawa)
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix khoya, all-purpose flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add a little milk and knead into a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small, crack-free balls.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan on low flame.
  5. Fry the balls until golden brown, stirring gently.
  6. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom powder.
  7. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour.
  8. Serve gulab jamun warm or at room temperature.

Butter Paratha

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ghee or oil (for cooking)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix whole wheat flour, salt, and water to make a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls.
  3. Roll each ball into a flat circle using a rolling pin.
  4. Spread butter on the rolled dough and fold it.
  5. Roll again into a circle or triangle.
  6. Heat a tawa and cook the paratha on both sides.
  7. Apply ghee or oil while cooking until golden brown.
  8. Remove from tawa and spread more butter on top.
  9. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
